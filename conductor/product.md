# Product Definition - Recipe API

## Vision
A secure and intuitive REST API designed for home cooks to organize recipes, plan meals, and preserve family culinary traditions across generations.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Home cooks focused on family-centric meal planning.
- **Goal:** To provide a reliable platform for organizing daily meals while ensuring family recipes and traditions are preserved and accessible.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Tradition Preservation:** Focused on the long-term storage and collaborative management of family recipes.
- **Family Collaboration:** Facilitating shared meal planning and recipe contribution within a family unit.
- **Privacy First:** Ensuring that sensitive family data and personal recipes remain private and under the owner's control.

## Key Functional Features
- **Recipe Management:** CRUD operations for recipes, including components, ingredients, instructions, and versioning.
- **Meal Planning:** Flexible meal templates and scheduling capabilities.
- **Collaborative Family Groups:** (Planned) Secure sharing and co-authoring of recipes within defined family units.
- **Shared Meal Planning:** (Planned) Collaborative tools for families to plan and manage their weekly meals together.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- **Data Privacy & Ownership:** Robust security measures to protect family data and clear mechanisms for data export.
- **High Availability:** A reliable backend that remains accessible for active kitchen use and planning sessions.
- **Ease of Use:** A clean and well-documented API designed to power intuitive user interfaces for family members of all technical levels.
